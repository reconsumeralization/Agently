### 2.1 Bid and Procurement Statements / 投标与采购场景声明

For bid/procurement projects, trademark usage is managed by project amount (to the extent permitted by applicable law):

- If the declared project amount is `<= USD 10,000` (or equivalent currency), factual and verifiable text-only reference to Agently is allowed without fee.
- If the declared project amount is `> USD 10,000` (or equivalent currency), any use of Agently marks in bid/procurement materials requires prior written authorization and a paid trademark license from AgentEra.

Examples of use covered in the `> USD 10,000` (or equivalent currency) category include:

- textual references used for scoring/qualification/supporting materials
- compatibility or capability claims using Agently marks
- any use of Agently logo, icon, or visual branding in bid materials

Amount calculation rules:

- amount means the tax-inclusive total amount declared in bid/procurement documents
- split procurements for the same project phase and purchaser may be combined for threshold evaluation
- for non-USD bids, conversion uses a publicly available exchange rate on the bid closing date (or the previous business day if unavailable)

在招投标、询价、采购场景中，商标使用按项目金额管理（在适用法律允许范围内）：

- 若申报项目金额 `<= 10,000 美元或等额货币`，可免费进行真实、可核验的文字性引用。
- 若申报项目金额 `> 10,000 美元或等额货币`，凡在投标/采购材料中使用 Agently 商标，均需事先书面授权并签署有偿商标许可。

`> 10,000 美元或等额货币`场景中，以下使用均纳入授权范围：

- 用于评分、资质或支撑材料的文字引用
- 使用 Agently 商标作兼容性或能力背书描述
- 在标书材料中使用 Agently Logo、图标或视觉品牌元素

金额口径：

- 以投标/采购文件中申报的含税总金额为准
- 同一项目阶段、同一采购主体的拆分采购，可合并判断是否触发阈值
- 非美元报价按投标截止日（如无则以前一工作日）公开汇率折算为美元判断阈值
