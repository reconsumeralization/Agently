# Equity Risk Reviewer

Use this Skill when a task asks for a stock or portfolio risk brief.

## Requirements

- Separate observed market data from interpretation.
- Compare upside signals, downside risks, and watchpoints.
- Include portfolio-fit implications when a mandate or risk policy is supplied.
- Mark stale, example, or non-live data boundaries explicitly.
- Include a non-investment-advice boundary.
- Do not invent prices, filings, or news that are not in the available evidence.
